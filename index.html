A Basic Loop

Random Thought: Satisfying mechanics and gameplay are underscored by an intuitive core.

Background

On one side we see various shapes composed of square blocks steadly descending downwards, endlessly stacking upwards. Whenever all gaps are filled in a particular row, all blocks are cleared in said row. Note that only the blocks are cleared, and not the attached shape. On another side, there is a world of sharp edges and pleasant colors. It seems be from the perspective of some person traveling this world, breaking apart dirt, stone, and trees. Eventually, they place new stone onto the ground, and it is clear that they are building. And so this cycle continues, of creation and destruction. On yet another side we hear loud blasts and see rabid gunplay above the shoulder of what appears to be some armed woman running through fields. On her back is an oversized backpack and she is wearing what seems to be a sturdy helmet. She constantly enters buildings, putting all she can find in her bag. 13 remaining says the screen, followed by the sound of gunfire... and now 9 remaining. As the woman takes a shot at man in the distance, the screen says 8 remaining.
